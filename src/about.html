About

This website was created as a part of an educational website to provide the information about Banh Mi - a famous Vietnamese sandwich. The purpose is to introduce readers about the history, many types, cultural significane, and recipe of Banh Mi for food lovers who wants to make Banh Mi at home.

Author Information

"Hi! My name is Duong Truong Phuc Nguyen. I am a food lover, so I love to explore many different food cultures around the world. For the educational website, I chose Banh Mi as my favorite food and it's a mixture of French baguette and Vietnamese sandwich. I hope that my website will help everyone to get more information of this food and inspire them to try it!"
